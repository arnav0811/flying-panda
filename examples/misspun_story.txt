Yet, amid the chaos, his mind was never far from the lab. The impulsive decision to wipe down the bench left smudged traces of the neurotoxin—a desperate attempt to erase his involvement. He ignored the gnawing dread that crept into his mind, hoping that if he acted normal, no one would suspect him. 

As he waited for the sun to rise, Michael felt a weight settle upon him, a sense of impending doom that threatened to unravel his carefully crafted façade. In that moment, he realized he was already trapped in a web of his own making, one that would soon ensnare him in questions he could never answer convincingly enough.
